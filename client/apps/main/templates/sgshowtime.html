SG Showtime

SG Showtime provides movie showtimes for all major cinemas in Singapore. It also lets you watch movie trailers, find cinemas near you, and book movie tickets.

Showtimes for all cinemas.

SG Showtime provides movie showtimes for all major cinemas in Singapore, including Cathay, Golden Village, Shaw, and Filmgarde.

New releases and upcoming.

SG Showtime provides detailed movie info for both new releases and upcoming movies. The movie info includes director, cast, language, genre and synopsis.

Check seats and book tickets.

Check seats and book movie tickets online from Cathay, Golden Village, Shaw, and Filmgarde.

Watch Trailers

SG Showtime lets you watch movie trailers right inside the app. Enjoy the high-definition movie trailers at home or on the go.

Nearby Cinemas

Want to find a cinema near you? SG Showtime shows you a list of nearby cinemas sorted by distance.

Updated Daily

SG Showtime updates its movies and showtimes data every day, so you always have the latest information.
