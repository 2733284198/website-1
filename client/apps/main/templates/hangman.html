Little Hangman

Little Hangman is a modern rendition of the classic Hangman game, lovingly created just for kids.

Beautiful themes.

Little Hangman comes with four beautifully designed themes: blackboard, graph paper, meadow and brick wall.

Carefully selected word lists.

We hand-picked word lists that are kids-friendly. The word lists cover K-6 words, animals, plants and many other cateogies.

No score, no timer, no pressure.

Playing a word puzzle game should be fun. Win or lose, you will be delighted by joyful jingles and lovely animations.

Challenge Mode

In the challenge mode you can enter a word then let another player guess it. It's a lot of fun for parents to play with kids.

Portrait and Landscape

Little Hangman is designed for both portrait and landscape modes. Hold you iPad any way you want, and the game always looks great.

Kids Safe

Little Hangman does not have in-app purchases or ads. It does not collect any information about the user. You can be rest assured that it's safe for kids to play.
