Little Hangman

Little Hangman is a modern rendition of the classic Hangman game, lovingly created just for kids.

Beautiful themes.

Little Hangman comes with four beautifully designed themes: blackboard, meadow, graph paper and brick wall.

Carefully selected word lists.

We hand-picked word lists that are kids friendly, which cover K-6 words, animals, plants and many other cateogies.

No score, no timer, no pressure.

Playing a word puzzle game should be fun. No matter you get the word right or not, you will be greeted with joyful jingles and animations.

Challenge Mode

In the challenge mode you can enter a word then let another player guess it. It's a lot of fun for parents to play with kids.

Portrait and Landscape

Little Hangman is designed for both portrait and landscape modes. Hold you iPad any way you want, and Little Hangman always looks great.

Kids Safe

Little Hangman does not have any in-app purchase or ads. It does not collect any information about the user, and does not send anything over the Internet. You can be rest assured that it's safe for kids to play.
