bus@sg

bus@sg provides real-time bus arrival prediction for SBS and SMRT bus routes in Singapore.

Save to favourites.

Save bus stops to your favourites for easy access. You can also enable auto-loading for individual routes plying through a favourite stop.

Find nearby stops.

bus@sg shows you a list of bus stops near your current location, sorted by distance.

Map and search.

bus@sg has a full-size map with integrated search. You can quickly find a bus stop by its name, stop id or road name.

Routes

You can browse all the bus routes in Singapore, and look up the list of stops each route plies.

Schedules

bus@sg also provides full schedules for each route, including first bus time, last bus time and frequencies.

Up-to-date

bus@sg updates bus route automatically, so you always have the latest information.
